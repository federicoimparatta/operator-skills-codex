# Meeting brief: [topic]

**Date and time:** When.
**Duration:** Minutes.
**Attendees:** Names and roles.
**Prepared for:** User.
**Last updated:** Date.

---

## Outcome

One sentence. Decision, alignment, information, or relationship.

## Stakes

One to two sentences. What is won if this goes well. What is lost if it does not.

## Stakeholders

One subsection per speaking stakeholder.

### [Name, role]

- **Wants.** Their interest.
- **Fears.** What they are guarding against.
- **Knows.** Context they have.
- **Does not know.** Context missing.
- **Typical move.** How they usually behave in meetings like this.

## History

- Prior meeting. Date. Outcome. Link.
- Prior commitment. Owner. Status.
- Recent relevant message or thread. Link.
- Open action items owed by attendees.

## Opening move

Three beats or a short script.

1. Frame.
2. Signal of respect for time.
3. Ask or handoff.

## Materials

- Artifact. Purpose. Whether pre-read or live.

## Exits

- **Too long.** Cut plan.
- **Off-topic.** Redirect.
- **Emotional or hostile.** Holding or closing move.
- **Stalled decision.** Fallback ask.

## After

- **Notes.** Writer, recipients, deadline.
- **Actions.** Owner, deadline per action.
- **Absent stakeholders.** Follow-up plan.
