## 1. Recommendation

One of: hire, no-hire, hire for a different role, hire at a different level.

## 2. Summary

Two sentences. The reason for the recommendation, in direct terms.

## 3. Scorecard

Per rubric dimension, the consolidated score from the panel.

| Dimension | Score | Bar | Above or below | One-sentence evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Judgment under ambiguity | 4 | 3 | above | Revised a shaky answer into a clean one when pushed on assumption X. |
| Delivery track record | 3 | 3 | at | Named specific shipped work and the trade-offs, but one item was inflated on review with the reference. |
| Technical depth vs breadth | 4 | 3 | above | Deep on core domain, picked up adjacent topic Y in real time. |
| Ownership signal | 3 | 3 | at | Uses "we" and "I" accurately; did not take the easy blame lines. |
| Failure fluency | 2 | 3 | below | Discussed one failure directly, reframed another as "a learning experience" without the learning. |
| Scope compression | 4 | 3 | above | Pattern of shipping less than planned and winning. |
| Communication density | 4 | 3 | above | High signal across all rounds. |
| Political literacy | 3 | 3 | at | Anticipated pushback in case study. |
| Learning velocity | 4 | 3 | above | Sharpened answer inside interview on topic Z. |
