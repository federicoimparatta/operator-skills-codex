# Decline message

Paste directly into the ticket comment or the reporter's inbox. Adjust tone to match the product's voice, but keep the four parts.

---

Hi [reporter name],

Thanks for filing this. We are not going to do it.

[One sentence with the reason. Honest, short. Examples:
"The audience affected is narrow enough that the fix would displace higher-impact work."
"The workaround in the current product is acceptable for the use case."
"This scope belongs to [other team], and they track it separately."
"We have tried a version of this in the past and the result did not justify the cost."]

[One sentence with what would change this, or an explicit "there is no scenario we expect to reconsider". Examples:
"If we see this reported by more than five customers in a quarter, we would revisit."
"If this becomes a blocker for any paying tier above [name], we would revisit."
"We do not expect to reconsider this."]

[If an alternative exists, one sentence. Examples:
"In the meantime, [existing feature] handles the adjacent use case."
"If you can share the underlying workflow, we might find a closer fit among existing features."]

Closing the ticket now. Happy to discuss if the context changes.

[Your name]

---

## Guidelines

- Never use "we will consider it" without a concrete trigger.
- Never use "low priority" as a reason. That is not a reason, that is a deferral.
- Never cc stakeholders who were not on the original ticket. A private concern gets a private answer.
- Never invite the reporter to "advocate for this" or "make the case". That shifts product work to the user.
