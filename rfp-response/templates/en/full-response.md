# Response to [RFP name]

**RFP reference:** Number or code.
**Issuer:** Buyer entity.
**Vendor:** Legal entity submitting.
**Submission date:** Date.
**Language:** Language of the submission.

---

## 0. Cover letter

One page. Who we are, what the buyer asked, that we comply, and the named contact for this response.

## 1. Executive summary

One to two pages. What we propose. Why it fits the buyer's problem. The three points the evaluator should remember if they only read this section.

## 2. Compliance matrix

See `compliance-matrix.md`. Included in full, not summarized.

## 3. Understanding of the buyer's problem

What the buyer is trying to achieve, in the buyer's words where possible. The constraints the buyer operates under. The evaluation criteria the buyer has named.

## 4. Technical approach

One subsection per major requirement or work area.

### 4.1. [Area name]

- **Buyer's need.** As stated.
- **Our decision.** What we propose.
- **Rationale.** Why this over alternatives.
- **Evidence.** Prior work, references, data.
- **Risks and mitigations.** Named openly.

## 5. Team

- **Roles committed.** Role, FTE allocation, replaceability.
- **Named individuals.** Only those who will work on the engagement.
- **Governance.** How decisions are made and escalated.

## 6. References

- **Reference 1.** Client, scope, value, duration, contactable name and role.
- **Reference 2.** …
- **Reference 3.** …

Prefer same-sector, same-scale, same-team references.

## 7. Delivery plan

- **Phases.** Start condition, end condition, deliverable per phase.
- **Milestones.** Dates or event-based gates.
- **Dependencies from buyer.** Named, with owners.

## 8. Pricing

Match the format demanded by the RFP.

- **Price elements.** What is covered.
- **Exclusions.** What is not covered.
- **Change triggers.** What would move the price, and how.

## 9. Risks and assumptions

- **Assumptions.** That must hold for the price and timeline.
- **Risks.** Named, with mitigation.

## 10. Legal and commercial terms

- Requested modifications to the RFP's contract template, if any, with justification.
- Insurance, liability, confidentiality.

## 11. Appendices

- A. Compliance matrix (full).
- B. Team CVs.
- C. Reference case studies.
- D. Certifications and attestations.
- E. Any other mandatory attachment named by the RFP.
